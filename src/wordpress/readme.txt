== Description ==

Provides a shortcode that allows nonprofits to display the Better Giving donation form.
Shortcode takes user-defined attributes, allowing users the ability to customize the form's look and feel.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do I need to sign up for an account to use the Better Giving Donation Form for my nonprofit?  =

Yes, you can sign up your nonprofit for a free account on Better Giving. Visit our [](http://better.giving/register)

= Do I have to accept certain payment options? =

Not at all! The donation form has arguments that allow you complete control over the payment options that you want to show to your donors. This is setup on a shortcode basis, allowing you to offer a payment type on one form and other types on another where it is more appropriate.

Check out out donation form [documentation page](https://better.giving/wp-docs) to find out more.
